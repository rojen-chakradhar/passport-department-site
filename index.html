How to Apply for a Passport in Nepal?

A simple, step-by-step informational guide to help Nepali citizens understand the passport application process.

Disclaimer

This website is for informational purposes only. Always refer to official government sources for the latest updates.

    How to apply for an Ordinary Passport?

    A. Start the application process bt filling the online application form (pre-enrollment form). Start the application process by filling the online application form (pre-enrollment form).

    The online form can be filled at home using a computer or mobile device. It is recommended to fill the form by the applicant him/herself, ensuring all the details are accurate and consistent with your official documents. While filling out the form, select the appropriate enrollment center such as District/Area Administration Office (for normal service) or the Department of Passports (for urgent service), or the concerned/relevant embassy (if you are living abroad. Then, select a suitable date and time for your appointment at the selected enrollment center.) After that, fill all the information in the given fields accurately, upload necessary documents (citizenship for new and citizenship certificate and previous for renew) and submit the form. After the submission, you'll receive a complete copy of the form with a barcode and QR code. Print the form and bring it when you go to the enrollment Center at your scheduled time. If printing the form isn't possible, save the form(pdf, not a screenshot)< on your mobile and present it during your appointment

    B. After completing the form application, visit your chosen enrollment center with the form and other necessary documents (original documents) to register your passport application. The application process includes in the following order:

      1. Form verification.

      2. Revenue payment.

      3. Live enrollment (Taking photograph and providing biometric information).

    C. Once the passport is ready, you will be notified via email or SMS (within Nepal only).

    Upon receiving the notification, bring the necessary documents (application form, revenue payment slip and previous passport) to the same location where you submitted your application to collect your passport.

    D. The above-mentioned process is applicable for all passport services, including new applications, renewals, and replacements due to loss, theft, or damage.

    Where to apply for a passport?

    A. Regular Service:

    If you are in Nepal, you should apply at the District Administration Office (DAO) or Area Administration Office (AAO) from where your citizenship certificate or minor identity card was issued. Typically, passports applied through these offices are issued within 10 to 30 days. The exact duration may vary depending on the distance from the Department of Passports and the available transportation facilities.

    B. Emergency Service:

    If you require a passport urgently, you can apply directlyat the Department of Passports in Tripureshwor, Kathmandu. Under this service, passports are typically issued within two working days after enrollment. For urgent service additional fees apply in the following manner. NPR 7,000 for a 34-page passport and NPR 12,000 for a 66-page passport.

    Special Provision for first time applications for children born abroad to Nepali parents

    Obtain a birth registration or temporary registration number. Children born around to Nepali parents who do not prossess a Minor Identity Card can apply for a passport through the respective Nepali diplomatic mission. In such cases, the embassy or consulate will raise a birth registration certificate or assign a temporary registration number. This number should be entered in the 'Citizenship Number' field of the passport application form. Requried Documents:
      1. Copies of Nepali citizenship certificates of both parents of the child. 2. Copies of passports of both parents. 3. Marriage registration certificate of parents. 4. Original birth registration certificate of the child. 5. Two recent passport-size photographs of the child.

    After submitting the above documents to the embassy, the embassy will provide the birth registration or temporary registration number. In the passport application form of the minor, the registration number provided by the embassy should be mentioned in place of the citizenship number.
    Please note that once a passport has been issued under this provision, obtaining a Minor Identity Card from the relevant District or Area Administration Office in Nepal is mandatory for any future passport renewals.

    Passport Application Process:
    Procedure of applying for an e-passport:

    1. To fill out the online pre-enrollment form for an e-passport, the following steps must be followed:

    (a) First of all fill out the online application form, and book the date and time of appointment. To fill the form, please click here (b) Then, four options will appear:
      (a) To apply for a passport for the first time (New) (b) To renew an expired passport (Renewal) (c) To obtain a passport in place of a lost/stolen passport (Replacement - Lost/Stolen) (d) To obtain passport in place of a damaged passport (Damaged)
    (c) After that, select whether you want to apply for a 34-page or 66-page passport and click the "Proceed" button (d) Then, choose the location from where you are applying. If applying from abroad, select "Other" option and choose your country of birth.
      Fill out all requried details step-by-step in the application form and upload the specified/required documents. Note: When uploading documents, scan the original documents and ensure that the file size is 300 KB or less.
    (e) After completing the entire form, review all the details to ensure they are accurate and match the uploaded supporting documents. Then, click save and submit the form online. (f) After submission, a "Download PDF" option will appear. Download the form and save the digital copy. Print the downloaded form and visit the enrollment center at the scheduled time. If you do not have a printing facility, you can also save the form on your mobile phone and bring it with you. However, when bringing it on your mobile, make sure to bring the original downloaded form (PDF) not a screenshot. Note: If there is any error in your printed passport, a new application fee must be submitted with the applicable fee. Therefore, fill out the details carefully and accurately. It is best to fill it out the details yourself, and if someone else is assiting, make sure to review the details yourself. Also, if you find any error submitting the form, you can rewuest for correction at the time your photo is taken. After making corrections, review all the details properly before signing the form. Once your photo is taken and the form is approved, corrections cannot be made. If an error is found afterward, the passprt must be cancelled, and the entire process must be started again from the beginning.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

    How long does it take to get an electronic passport after doing live enrollment in the districts?

    Passports will be available in all the three districts of Kathmandu Valley within a week. Passports will be available within 15 days on the Mahendra Highway outside Kathmandu and surrounding districts and districts with air facilities. After receiving the passport at te district administration officies, an SMS will be sent to the applicant's mobile to collect the passport.

    From where can we apply for regular ePassport services?

    The regular service for ePassport is available from all the District Administration Offices in Nepal and all Nepali Diplomatic Missions abroad.

    How long does it take to get an electronic passport after doing live enrollment in the districts?

    Passports will be available in all the three districts of Kathmandu Valley within a week. Passports will be available within 15 days on the Mahendra Hightway outside Kathmandu and surrounding districts and districts with air facilities. After receiving the passport at the district administration offices, an SMS will be sent to the applicant's mobile to collect the passport.

    My citizenship is longer than 14 digits. How am I supposed to fill in pre-enrollment form?

    While filling the citizenship number, applicants are required to fill the number from right side incase citizenship number is longer than 14 digits. For eg: if your citizenship number is 072-312-13792/432796, please fill 31213792432796. Note that current system does not allow special characters like hyphen(-) or slash(/), and hence is not requried.

    What to do if error is discovered in an already received passport?

    If there is an error in an already received passport, you will need to apply for a new passport. To do this, you must complete the entire process again and pay the applicable fee as per the rules.

    While renewing a passport, is the renewed passport made with old passport number? I have a visa in my passport, what will happen if renewed?

    No, when you renew your passport, a new passport number will be issued. However, since the old passport number is mentioned in the passport, both old and new passports can be carried together for travel, and the visa in the old passport is generally valid. However, since this depends on the rules of the destination country, it is advisable to check with the official authorities of that country.
